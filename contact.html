Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for classes?

You can register for classes by filling out the contact form on this page, calling our phone number, or sending an email. Once we receive your inquiry, we'll guide you through the registration process and provide payment information.

What is your cancellation policy?

For private lessons, we request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Lessons canceled with less than 24 hours notice may be charged the full lesson fee. For group classes, we offer a prorated refund if you need to withdraw from a class series before the halfway point.

Do you offer trial lessons?

Yes! We offer a discounted trial lesson for new students to ensure we're a good fit before committing to a package. For group classes, you can also attend the first session of a series before deciding to continue.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and major credit cards. For regular students, we offer the option of setting up automatic monthly payments for convenience.

How long does it take to get a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. If you need an immediate response, please call our phone number during office hours.
