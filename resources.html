Puzzle of the Week

White to Move and Win

Difficulty: Intermediate

Each week, I post a new chess puzzle for you to solve. These puzzles are carefully selected to help you develop your tactical vision and calculation skills. Try to solve them without moving the pieces first, then use the interactive board to check your solution.

Recommended Resources

Books for Different Levels

For Beginners

  • "Bobby Fischer Teaches Chess" by Bobby Fischer
  • "Chess Fundamentals" by José Raúl Capablanca
  • "Logical Chess: Move by Move" by Irving Chernev

For Intermediate Players

  • "My System" by Aron Nimzowitsch
  • "Winning Chess Strategies" by Yasser Seirawan
  • "How to Reassess Your Chess" by Jeremy Silman

For Advanced Players

  • "Dvoretsky's Endgame Manual" by Mark Dvoretsky
  • "The Art of Attack in Chess" by Vladimir Vukovic
  • "Positional Chess Handbook" by Israel Gelfer
