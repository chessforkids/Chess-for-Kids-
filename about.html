My Teaching Philosophy

Critical Thinking

I believe chess is one of the best tools for developing critical thinking skills. My lessons emphasize the thought process behind moves rather than just memorization.

Individual Approach

Every student is unique. I tailor my teaching methods to match each student's learning style, interests, and goals to ensure maximum engagement and progress.

Measurable Progress

I believe in setting clear goals and tracking progress. My structured curriculum ensures students can see their improvement over time.

Enjoyment First

Chess should be fun! I create a positive, encouraging environment where students develop a genuine love for the game that motivates continued learning.
