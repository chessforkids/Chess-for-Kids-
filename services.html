Chess Coaching Services

Tailored instruction to help you reach your chess goals

My Coaching Approach

I offer a range of chess coaching services designed to meet the needs of players at all levels. My teaching methodology combines fundamental chess principles with personalized guidance, focusing on both tactical and strategic elements of the game. Whether you're just starting out or looking to take your game to the next level, I have a program that's right for you.

Individual Lessons

One-on-one instruction is the most effective way to improve your chess skills rapidly. These personalized sessions are tailored specifically to your strengths, weaknesses, and goals.

What to Expect

Each individual lesson begins with a brief review of previous material and homework, followed by focused instruction on specific aspects of your game that need improvement. We'll analyze your games, work through instructive positions, and develop a personalized study plan.

Group Classes

Group classes offer a collaborative learning environment where students can learn from both the instructor and their peers. These structured sessions follow a curriculum designed for specific skill levels.

Class Structure

Each group class includes a lesson on a specific chess topic, interactive exercises, guided practice, and homework assignments. Classes are kept small (maximum 8 students) to ensure everyone receives adequate attention.

Available Group Classes

  • Beginner Class: For those new to chess or with limited experience. Covers basic rules, piece movement, simple tactics, and fundamental principles.
  • Intermediate Class: For players familiar with the basics who want to improve. Covers tactical patterns, basic endgames, opening principles, and strategic concepts.
  • Advanced Class: For experienced players looking to refine their skills. Covers complex tactics, advanced endgame theory, opening repertoire development, and positional play.

Online Coaching

Online coaching offers the flexibility to learn from anywhere with an internet connection. These sessions use specialized chess platforms and video conferencing to provide an interactive learning experience.

Online Session Format

Online coaching sessions utilize screen sharing, interactive chess boards, and video conferencing to create an engaging learning environment. We'll use platforms like chess.com, lichess.org, or ChessBase to analyze positions, play training games, and work through exercises together.

Technical Requirements

  • Stable internet connection
  • Computer, tablet, or smartphone
  • Webcam and microphone (recommended but not required)
  • Free account on chess.com or lichess.org

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you teach?

I work with students of all ages, from young children (as young as 5 years old) to seniors. My teaching approach is adapted to suit the learning style and needs of each age group.

Do I need any prior chess knowledge to start lessons?

Not at all! I welcome complete beginners and will teach you everything from the basic rules to advanced concepts at a pace that works for you. For those with some experience, we'll assess your current level and build from there.

What materials do I need for lessons?

For in-person lessons, a chess set is provided, but having your own for practice at home is recommended. A notebook for taking notes is also helpful. For online lessons, you'll need a device with internet access and possibly a free account on chess.com or lichess.org.

How often should I take lessons?

For optimal progress, I recommend weekly lessons, which provides enough time to practice between sessions without losing momentum. However, I offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate different needs and availability.

Do you offer trial lessons?

Yes! I offer a discounted trial lesson for new students to ensure we're a good fit before committing to a package. This gives you a chance to experience my teaching style and ask any questions you might have.

How do I track my progress?

I provide regular progress assessments for all students. For online players, we can track rating progress on chess platforms. For all students, I maintain notes on strengths, weaknesses, and areas of improvement that we review periodically.
